Traffic rules

Vehicles in Japan are driven on the left-hand side of the road.

A red light means "Stop". Turning right/left is prohibited.

When a green arrow appears, cars can proceed in the direction of the green arrow even if the light is yellow or red.

Speed limits are assigned to all roads, including general roads and toll roads. Please observe the speed limit and drive safely. Also, be aware that some roads are monitored by automated speed monitoring systems. Be careful not to speed!

Driving under the influence of alcohol is prohibited by law. Use of a cell phone or smart phone while driving is prohibited by law.

Vehicles in Japan are driven on the left-hand side of the road. But please try not too close to the left side.

In case of the accident

Insurance will not cover and compensation will not be paid when the necessary procedures are not completed.

Call an ambulance (dial 119) to help injured person(s). After helping injured parson(s),move the rental vehicle to the safe area as quickly as possible.

Call the police (dial 110) from the scene. Both victim of and responsible for the accident have to report to the police. Please complete necessary procedures and keep “The accident certificate” with you.

Call the departure outlet from the scene. If the departure outlet closed, contact “Accident Reception Center” on Somopo Japan Nipponkoa Insurance Inc.
